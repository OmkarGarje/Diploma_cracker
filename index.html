About the Project :-

Diploma Cracker is an innovative platform developed by students pursuing a Diploma in Computer Technology(CM) at Bharati Vidyapeeth Institute of Technology, Navi Mumbai. The website is designed to address everyday academic challenges faced by both students and teachers, making it easier for them to access and share valuable resources.

For students, the platform offers organized access to study materials and important questions from previous years, allowing them to prepare more effectively. An interactive Q&A feature further enhances the learning experience by enabling students to ask questions directly to high-achieving "toppers" for additional guidance and support.

Teachers benefit from a streamlined process for sharing notes, presentations, and other resources, simplifying communication with their classes and ensuring that essential materials are easily accessible to all students.

The platform also categorizes subjects into coding and theory, allowing students to easily access all PowerPoint presentations for each unit. Model answers are available as well, providing students with valuable resources to enhance their understanding and exam preparation.

The website also includes a built-in Study Timer aimed at helping students stay focused. By setting structured, timed study sessions, students can better manage their schedules, reduce distractions, and maintain concentration.
Overall, Diploma Cracker is designed to be a comprehensive tool that supports both learning and teaching, fostering academic growth and efficiency for diploma students and teachers alike.
